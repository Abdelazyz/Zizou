About

I am a PhD candidate at GIPSA-Lab (Grenoble INP) working in collaboration with ENGIE Green through a CIFRE industrial thesis. My research focuses on deep learning methods for acoustic signal processing and wind turbine noise estimation.

My work explores the impact of environmental data on spectrogram reconstruction and noise level prediction using recurrent neural networks and data-driven modeling approaches.

I am particularly interested in AI-driven acoustic modeling, environmental sound analysis, and large-scale data processing.
